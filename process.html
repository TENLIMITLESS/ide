Virtual IdeaThon Process

Our virtual IdeaThon is designed to be inclusive and accessible to everyone, no matter where you are. Here’s a step-by-step guide:

1

Registration

Participants register online through our Google Form. Make sure to fill out all required fields to complete the registration.

2

Team Formation

Form teams of up to 5 members. If you don't have a team, we can help you find one through our online platform.

3

Kick-off

Join our virtual kick-off meeting where we'll discuss the rules, themes, and judging criteria. Get ready to start brainstorming!

4

Development

Work on your projects over the course of 14 days. You can use any technology stack you prefer. Our mentors are available to assist you.

5

Mentorship

Mentors will be available online to provide guidance and support for any technical or design challenges you may face.

6

Submission

Submit your project via our online portal. You'll need to provide a link to your code repository and a short demo video showcasing your project.

7

Judging

Our panel of judges will review the submissions based on creativity, technical difficulty, and potential impact. Each submission will be thoroughly evaluated.

8

Awards Ceremony

Winners will be announced in a virtual awards ceremony. Prizes will be awarded to the top teams based on their project’s impact and innovation.
